Wooden Carriage

Motorized Gears and Linkages

My rotary-to-linear mechanism is a gear-powered carriage. There is a gear attached to a servo (which generates rotational motion), that turns a smaller gear, that is connected to axle that turns a large wheel that causes the carriage to move (output linear motion).
