RC Battle Tank

Take on a Wheel-less Vehicle

My partner and I designed and manufactured a remote-controlled tank capable of climbing steep hills and maneuvering through different terrains. The tank is controlled by phone via Bluetooth and can traverse forwards, backwards, left, and right. The two sprockets that turn the two 3D-printed treads are powered 4AA batteries and a gear motor for each side. An arduino controls the vehicleâ€™s overall logic flow. On top of all of this (literally), is a lightweight cosmetic shell, laser-cut from chipboard, protecting the vehicle from minor impacts. The shell can be easily removed from the electronic internals since it is attached with velcro. The tank also features interchangeable and replaceable treads; our group made ones with grip tape on the bottom to enhance traction, as well as low grip ones for higher turning and top speeds.
