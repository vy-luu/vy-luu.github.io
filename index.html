About me

Hello! I am fourth-year Mechanical Engineering major at UC Berkeley.

I have done several internships in Product Design. Most recently, I had the opportunity to work for six months in the Google Phones Team. Prior to that, I also interned for six months at Synapse Product Development, where I assisted with a variety of consumer products. In the past, I interned in wearables at Skylar and in public space design as a part of a Pop-Up Makerspace project.

In school, I have been a part of numerous extracurricular activities. I am most proud of my current involvement with CalSol (UC Berkeley's Solar Vehicle Team), but I also enjoyed my time as a part of Berkeley Innovation's Human-Centered Design Consultancy.

I love art and thinking of new things to create. The intersection of technology and art excites me!
