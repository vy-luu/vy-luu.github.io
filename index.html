About me

Hello! I am second-year studying Mechanical Engineering at UC Berkeley.

I am currently a Graphic Designer and Website Assistant for UC Berkeley's Division of Student Affairs: Learning and Development. On the side, I do UI/UX design and rapid prototyping with Berkeley Innovation, UC Berkeley's Human-Centered Design Consultancy. In the past, I was a Public Relations Officer for UC Berkeley's Society of Women Engineers. I also worked in a Mobile Makerspace, facilitating the recreation of public space design one summer.

I love hand-crafted projects, and spend most of my free-time thinking of new things I can create. My hobbies spurred an interest in combining technology and art. More than anything, I want to see my creations wow people.
