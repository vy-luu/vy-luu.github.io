About me

Hello! I am third-year studying Mechanical Engineering at UC Berkeley.

I am currently a Mechanical Engineering Intern at Synapse Product Development, an undergraduate researcher for UC Berkeley's Center for the Built Enviroment, and a member of UC Berkeley's CalSol Composites and Operations Team.

I was previously a Graphic Designer and Website Assistant for UC Berkeley's Division of Student Affairs: Learning and Developmentfor two years. On the side, I was a product consultant for UC Berkeley's Bay Area Engineering Consultancy. I have also done UI/UX design and rapid prototyping with Berkeley Innovation, UC Berkeley's Human-Centered Design Consultancy. I was also a Public Relations Officer for UC Berkeley's Society of Women Engineers. For a short time, I worked in a Mobile Makerspace, facilitating the recreation of public space design one summer.

I love hand-crafted projects, and spend most of my free-time thinking of new things I can create. My hobbies spurred an interest in combining technology and art. More than anything, I want to see my creations wow people.
